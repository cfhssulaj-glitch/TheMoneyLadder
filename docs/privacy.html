Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Overview

The Money Ladder ("the App") is a personal finance planning tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Storage

All financial data you enter into the App (income, expenses, debts, savings) is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, collect, or store your financial information on any server.

No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in with any service.

Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription or lifetime access, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage purchase validation and subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt from Apple â€” it does not receive your financial data. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or crash reports.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used by the App is RevenueCat for in-app purchase management. No other third-party services receive data from the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
